Caramel Macchiato

Freshly steamed milk with vanilla-flavored syrup is marked with espresso and topped with caramel drizzle.

Description: A sweet and creamy coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, vanilla syrup, and topped with caramel drizzle, offering a delightful blend of flavors.

Tasting Notes: Rich and creamy with layers of vanilla and espresso flavors. The caramel adds a sweet, buttery note, while the espresso provides a robust foundation.

Brewing Methods: Best prepared with an espresso machine for brewing the espresso shot. Steamed milk can be frothed using a steam wand, and vanilla syrup can be added before layering the espresso on top. For a home-friendly version, you can use a Moka pot for espresso and heat milk using a stovetop or microwave.

Cappuccino

Description: A classic coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a rich and balanced beverage with a delightful texture.

Iced White Mocha

Espresso combined with white mocha sauce and milk over ice. Topped with whipped vanilla topping.

Description: A sweet and creamy coffee beverage made with espresso, milk, and white chocolate sauce, served over ice for a refreshing treat.
